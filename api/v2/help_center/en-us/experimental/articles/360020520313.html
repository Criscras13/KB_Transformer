As part of your security awareness training plan, we recommend that you require your users to complete their assigned security training. This means making training mandatory for all users.

Strengthening your human firewall will reduce the risk of your employees falling victim to phishing attacks.\u00a0Enrolling your users in security awareness training on a regular basis will prepare them with the skills that they need to be able to identify and report cybersecurity threats.
