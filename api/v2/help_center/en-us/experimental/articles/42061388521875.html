KnowBe4\u2019s Program Maturity Assessment (PMA) is a free tool that gives you quick insight into what level your organization ranks regarding its security culture and risk awareness. Your results will show you how to improve your organization\u2019s level. The PMA questionnaire is approximately five minutes long.\u00a0
\n
Getting Started
\n
To get started with your PMA, follow these steps below.
\n
    \n
  1. Go to the Program Maturity Assessment (PMA) page on KnowBe4\u2019s website.
    2. \n
  2. Enter your information, including your name, business email address, company name, and phone number, under the Get Your Maturity Assessment Now section at the right of the page.
    3. \n
  3. Click the Sign Up button.\n
      
    \n
    4. \n
  4. Within a few minutes, you should receive an email with the subject line Program Maturity Assessment. Check your Spam folder if you don\u2019t see the email in your primary inbox.
    5. \n
  5. In the email, click the Start Assessment button. The assessment should open in another browser tab.\n
      
    \n
    6. \n
  6. To begin the assessment, click the Start Questionnaire button.\n
      
    \n
    7. \n
\n
Taking the Assessment\u00a0
\n
The PMA will evaluate your organization across 40 maturity indicators in ten critical dimensions of human risk management:\u00a0
\n
    \n
  • \nLeadership and Strategy: Is cybersecurity a priority to leadership?
    • \n
  • \nAwareness and Behaviors: Is your training inclusive and relevant?
    • \n
  • \nEmployee-focused Security Tools: Have you implemented multi-factor authentication (MFA), password managers, and phishing reporting tools?
    • \n
  • \nPolicies and Procedures: Are your security policies clear and accessible?
    • \n
  • \nEmployee Mindset: Do your people take personal responsibility for security?
    • \n
  • \nMeasurement and Metrics: How do you track program effectiveness?
    • \n
  • \nContinuous Improvement: Does your program evolve based on new threats?
    • \n
  • \nEmployee Engagement: Do people actively participate in security initiatives?
    • \n
  • \nRisk Awareness: Do employees understand your organization's specific risks and the security impact of their actions?
    • \n
  • \nIntegration with Business: Is security incorporated into everyday processes?
    • \n
\n
In each of these ten sections, select your level of agreement with several statements, from Strongly Disagree to Strongly Agree. For example, view the Leadership and Strategy section below, the first of the PMA\u2019s ten sections:
\n
  
\n
When you complete the ten sections, your PMA score will be calculated, and an action plan will be customized for your organization.\u00a0
\n
Understanding Your Assessment
\n
The Assessment Results page provides insight into what level your organization ranks regarding its security culture and risk awareness.
\n
  
\n
The maximum score an organization can receive is 200, and there are five levels that could match your organization. The five levels are as follows, from lowest to highest:
\n
    \n
  1. \nBasic Compliance: The minimum security requirements to meet regulatory standards
    2. \n
  2. \nSecurity Awareness Foundation: Building awareness across the organization
    3. \n
  3. \nProgrammatic Security Awareness and Behavior: A structured program with measurable outcomes
    4. \n
  4. \nSecurity Behavior Management: Active management of security behaviors
    5. \n
  5. \nSustainable Security Culture: Security embedded in organizational DNA
    6. \n
\n
At the lowest level (Basic Compliance), your organization\u2019s risk of a security breach is the highest. However, as you increase your organization\u2019s human risk management practices, the likelihood of a security breach decreases.
\n
Your results will also include a spider diagram showing performance by section and horizontal bar graphs showing your scores in each of the ten sections.
\n
  
\n
  
\n
Under the Ready to Advance Your Maturity Level? section, you can download your full PMA report with an action plan customized for your organization by clicking the Download Full Report button. Your report will be downloaded as a PDF file. If you have any questions regarding your report, click the Contact Us button.
\n
  
\n
If you would like to retake the Program Maturity Assessment, click the Retake Assessment button at the bottom of the Assessment Results page.
\n
  
\n
Program Maturity Assessment Results Email
\n
Your results, including your full report with an action plan customized for your organization, will also be sent to the email address you previously provided. You can review the Assessment Results page again by clicking the Review Results Summary button, contact us if you have any questions, and retake the assessment via the link at the bottom of the email.
\n
