In this article, you'll learn how to integrate your ControlMap platform with your KSAT console. This integration will allow you to send training campaign data to ControlMap so that you can mark training requirements as complete.
\n
\nNote: Before you set up this integration, you'll need to copy the Reporting API Token from your KSAT Account Settings. For more information, see the API section of our KSAT Account Settings: Account Integrations article.
\n
To integrate ControlMap with KSAT, follow the steps below:
\n
    \n
  1. Log in to ControlMap.
    2. \n
  2. From the navigation panel, select\u00a0Integrations.
    3. \n
  3. In the top-right corner of the page, click + New Integration.
    4. \n
  4. Select\u00a0KnowBe4.
    5. \n
  5. In the\u00a0KnowBe4 API Bearer Token\u00a0field, paste your\u00a0Reporting API Token. \n\n
    6. \n
  6. Click Create. When you click this button, a\u00a0Training Campaigns drop-down menu will display.
    7. \n
  7. From the drop-down menu, select the training campaigns you would like to send data from.
    8. \n
  8. Click Update Connection.
    9. \n
  9. Review the checks that the connector will perform, then click Next.
    10. \n
  10. Set a schedule for your scan. On the frequency, day, and time you set, the connector will scan your selected training campaigns for data. \n\n
    11. \n
  11. To initiate a scan now, click Save & Scan Now. To wait to initiate a scan until the scheduled day, click Save Schedule instead.
    12. \n
\n
Once you configure the integration and perform a scan, your training campaign data will display in ControlMap. As your users complete their training for these campaigns, it will be marked as complete in ControlMap.
\n
To view your training campaign data, navigate to your KnowBe4 integration page and select the Awareness Training tab.
