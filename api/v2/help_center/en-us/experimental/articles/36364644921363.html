When deploying Defend to Microsoft 365, there are potential issues that may arise. These issues can prevent Defend from functioning correctly, leading to email delays or failures. It is essential to troubleshoot these issues promptly and efficiently to ensure a successful deployment.\u00a0
\n
This article highlights the common issues that may occur when deploying Defend to Microsoft 365 and how to manage them.\u00a0
\n
Mail Flow Issues\u00a0
\n
This issue occurs when there is a problem with the mail flow within Microsoft 365 at the time of the Defend deployment. This deployment may cause significant problems with correctly processing emails, resulting in a blocked mail flow to your organization.
\n
Resolution\u00a0
\n
To resolve this issue, check the mail flow configuration in Microsoft 365 to ensure it is correct. Verify that your mail flow rules are configured correctly and that there are no issues with email transport. Rerun the health check on the Defend console's\u00a0Settings\u00a0page to ensure that the issue is resolved. If the issue persists, contact support for further assistance.\u00a0
\n
Unknown Errors\u00a0
\n
These issues occur when an unknown error is encountered during the Defend deployment or the health check process. This issue may impact Defend's ability to process emails.\u00a0
\n
Resolution\u00a0
\n
To resolve this issue, gather more information about the error by checking Microsoft 365 event logs. Look for any error messages or codes that may indicate the cause of the issue. Then, contact support for further assistance, providing as much detail as possible about the error. Rerun the health check on the Defend console's Settings page to ensure that the issue is resolved.\u00a0
