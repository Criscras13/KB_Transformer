In KCM GRC, you can update your personal information and change your password from the User Profile page. By keeping your personal information up-to-date, other KCM GRC users in your organization will know how they can contact you.\u00a0
\n
To learn how to update your personal information and change your password, see the sections below.\u00a0
\n
\n
Updating Your Personal Information
\n
From the User Profile page, you can update your first name, last name, and phone number.\u00a0
\n
To update your personal information, follow the steps below:
\n
    \n
  1. Log in to your KCM GRC account.\u00a0
    2. \n
  2. At the top-right corner of the page, click your name.
    3. \n
  3. Click the\u00a0Profile button.\u00a0 \n
    4. \n
  4. Enter your personal information in the First Name,\u00a0Last Name, or\u00a0Telephone fields.\u00a0\n
    \n
    \nNote:If you need to update your email address, contact your Account Administrator. If you're an Account Administrator, you can update all email addresses, including your email address, from the Manage Users\u00a0page (Settings > Manage Users).
    \n
    \n
    5. \n
  5. Click the\u00a0Save button.\u00a0
    6. \n
\n
\n
Changing Your Password
\n
If you know your password and would like to change it, you can change your password from the User Profile page. If you forgot your password, you can reset it by following the instructions in the How Do I Reset My Password? section of our How to Activate Your Account, Access Your Account, and Reset Your Password article.\u00a0 \u00a0
\n
To change your password, follow the steps below:
\n
    \n
  1. Log in to your KCM GRC account.\u00a0
    2. \n
  2. At the top-right corner of the page, click your name.\u00a0
    3. \n
  3. Click the\u00a0Change Password button. When you click this button, you'll be taken to the\u00a0User Profile page.\u00a0 \n
    4. \n
  4. Enter your current password in the\u00a0Current Password field.
    5. \n
  5. Enter your new password in the\u00a0New Password and\u00a0Confirm New Password fields. The character limit for your password is 64 characters.
    6. \n
  6. Click the Save Password button.\u00a0
    7. \n
