At KnowBe4, we aim to provide the highest levels of security, availability, and reliability for our products. To accomplish this goal and keep our products up-to-date, scheduled maintenance is needed. Please note that certain product features may be unavailable during maintenance.
\n
To minimize potential downtime, we use tailored scheduled maintenance windows across global regions and aim for active maintenance to take two hours or less within those windows. Upcoming maintenance will be posted in advance on our status page at status.knowbe4.com.
\n
\n
\nTip:To be notified of scheduled maintenance, we recommend subscribing to status.knowbe4.com.
