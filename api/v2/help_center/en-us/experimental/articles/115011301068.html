As a reseller or managed service provider, you can export your customers\u2019 templates and landing pages to a file that can then be imported into another customer's account.
\n
You can use the Import and Export buttons in two areas of your accounts: the Phishing Templates and Landing Pages.
\n
To export and import content in your account, follow the steps below:
\n
    \n
  1. Log in to the customer\u2019s account where you would like to export templates.
    2. \n
  2. Navigate to Phishing > Phishing Templates > Managed Templates or Phishing > Landing Pages > Managed Landing Pages. 
    3. \n
  3. Select the check boxes next to the desired content. \n
    4. \n
  4. Click the Export button. This button will download a file to your downloads folder.\u00a0
    5. \n
  5. Next, navigate to the customer account where you would like to import the exported content and repeat step 2.
    6. \n
  6. Click the Import button. \n
    7. \n
  7. Click the Browse button and select the recently exported content in your computer's downloads folder. \n
    8. \n
  8. Next, click the Submit button to import your content. The content will be available in the customer account\u2019s Drafts category and will not be available for use until it is moved into a different category.
    9. \n
