In this article, you will learn how to integrate Cylance's endpoint protection platform (EPP) with SecurityCoach. Once you set up this integration, data provided by Cylance will be available under the SecurityCoach tab of your KSAT console. This data can be viewed in SecurityCoach reports and used to create detection rules for real-time coaching campaigns. For general information about SecurityCoach, see our SecurityCoach Product Manual.
\n
\n\n
\n
\nImportant:To configure the Cylance integration, you'll need access to a CylancePROTECT Administrator account.
\n
\n
Copy Your Organization Key
\n
Before you can set up this integration in your CylancePROTECT platform, you will need to authorize the configuration and copy your organization key from the SecurityCoach tab of your KSAT console. You will need this key in the Set Up the Integration in Your CylancePROTECT Platform section below.To locate and copy your organization key, follow the steps below:
\n
    \n
  1. Log in to your KSAT console and navigate to\u00a0SecurityCoach >\u00a0Setup > Security Vendor Integrations.
    2. \n
  2. Locate Cylance and click Configure.\n\n
    3. \n
  3. Click Authorize.\u00a0 \n\n
    4. \n
  4. In the pop-up window that opens, click OK.
    5. \n
  5. In the\u00a0Organization Key field, copy your key.\u00a0 \n\n
    6. \n
\n
Set Up the Integration in Your CylancePROTECT Platform
\n
Before you can set up this integration in your CylancePROTECT platform, you will need to authorize the configuration and copy your organization key from the SecurityCoach tab of your KSAT console.To set up the integration in your KSAT console, follow the steps below:
\n
    \n
  1. \u00a0Log in to your CylancePROTECT platform, and navigate to Settings > Application.\n
    \n
    \nImportant:You will need to log in as a user with an Administrator role.
    \n
    \n
    2. \n
  2. Scroll down to the\u00a0Integrations section of the page.
    3. \n
  3. Select the Syslog/SIEM check box. Selecting this check box will prompt Cylance to stream events to SecurityCoach.
    4. \n
  4. Fill out the remaining fields. For more information, see the screenshot and list below: \n
      \n
    1. \nEvent Types:\u00a0Select all check boxes except for the >Audit Log check box.
      2. \n
    2. \nSIEM: Leave this field blank.
      3. \n
    3. \nProtocol: From the drop-down menu, select the protocol that you prefer.
      4. \n
    4. \nTLS/SSL: Leave this check box unchecked to disable TLS/SSL.\n
      \n
      \nNote:This field will only display if you selected TCP in the Protocol field.
      \n
      \n
      5. \n
    5. \nIP/Domain: Enter the domain for your KnowBe4 instance into the field. To find the domain for your KnowBe4 instance, see the table below:\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n
      KnowBe4 InstanceDomain
      United Statessyslog.training.knowbe4.com
      European Unionsyslog.eu.knowbe4.com
      Canadasyslog.ca.knowbe4.com
      Germanysyslog.de.knowbe4.com
      United Kingdomsyslog.uk.knowbe4.com
      \n
      6. \n
    6. \nPort: Enter \"4514\" into the field.
      7. \n
    7. \nSeverity: From the drop-down menu, select Notice (5).
      8. \n
    8. \nFacility: From the drop-down menu, select Internal (5).\n
      9. \n
    9. \nCustom Token: Enter \"org_key=x,vendor_code_name=cylance,log_type=endpoint\", but replace \"x\" with your organization key.\u00a0
      10. \n
    \n\n
    5. \n
  5. Click Test Connection to confirm that Cylance is able to connect to SecurityCoach.
    6. \n
  6. Click SAVE.
    7. \n
\n
Map Your Users
\n
After you\u2019ve finished integrating Cylance, you can map your users either through mapping rules (recommended) or through a CSV file upload. For more information about user mapping, see our Mapping Users in SecurityCoach article.
\n
Once you\u2019ve successfully completed this integration, you can manage detection rules for Cylance on the Detection Rules subtab of SecurityCoach. For a full list of available system detection rules for this vendor, see our Which Detection Rules Can I Use with My Vendors? article.
\n
Delete the Integration in Your CylancePROTECT Platform
\n
To delete the Cylance integration from your SecurityCoach platform, follow the steps below.
\n
    \n
  1. Log in to your CylancePROTECT platform.
    2. \n
  2. Click the gear icon, and then click Application.
    3. \n
  3. Scroll down to the Integrations section of the page.
    4. \n
  4. Deselect the Syslog/SIEM check box.\n\n
