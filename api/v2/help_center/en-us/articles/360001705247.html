Sophos Firewalls
\n
Whitelisting in Sophos firewall allows users who've failed your phishing tests to access KnowBe4's landing pages.
\n
The instructions below were created for Sophos XG firewalls,\u00a0so other versions of Sophos firewalls may require a different set of steps. We recommend reaching out to Sophos for specific instructions on how to whitelist KnowBe4.
\n
To whitelist in Sophos XG firewalls:
\n
    \n
  1. \nContact support and request a copy of our phish domains and landing domains.
    2. \n
  2. Log in to the portal for the firewall.
    3. \n
  3. Click on Web, located on the left.
    4. \n
  4. Click on Exceptions, located at the top.
    5. \n
  5. If you don\u2019t have an exception list, click Add Exception.
    6. \n
  6. Provide a name (KnowBe4) and an optional description for the list.
    7. \n
  7. Check the boxes to the right under Skip the selected checks or actions for the services you purchased.
    8. \n
  8. Check URL pattern matches.
    9. \n
  9. Enter each phish and landing domain, one line at a time, in the Search/Add box. XXXXXX\u00a0and .com represent each phish and landing domain.^([A-Za-z0-9.-]\u200c*\\.)?XXXXXX\\.com\\.?/\n
    10. \n
  10. Click the Save button at the bottom of the page.
    11. \n
\n
\n
\nNote:After following this article, we recommend setting up a test phishing campaign to 1-2 users to ensure your whitelisting was successful. As a last resource, we suggest reaching out to your service provider for assistance. Visit here for an email template you can send to your service provider.
\n
\n
\n
\nTip:To ensure that you have whitelisted correctly, see our How to Verify You Have Whitelisted KnowBe4 Correctly article.
