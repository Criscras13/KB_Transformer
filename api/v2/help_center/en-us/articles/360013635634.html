In your Account Management console, you can edit your account profile and settings, manage your users, create phishing campaigns, and more.
\n
For a quick walkthrough of how to use your console, see our Management Console Overview video.
\n
Logging In
\n
You can log in to your Account Management console from your KnowBe4 Account Management login page. This page is the KnowBe4 instance where your console is hosted, followed by the subdirectory /management/login. See our Learner Support: KnowBe4's Training Instances and Login Links article for a full list of login links.
\n
\n
You can also log in to your Account Management console using SAML. For more information, see the Personal Settings section below.
\n
Partner Dashboard
\n
Once you log in to your account, you will see your Partner Dashboard page. Your Partner Dashboard page will display an overview of your subscription and the accounts you manage.For more information about this page, see the screenshot and list below:
\n
\n
    \n
  1. \nBulk Subscription Level: If you've purchased a bulk license of seats for the accounts you manage, you can view the subscription level. If you haven't purchased a bulk license of seats, this field will display N/A.
    2. \n
  2. \nBulk Subscription End Date: If you've purchased a bulk license of seats for the accounts you manage, you can view the end date for your subscription. If you haven't purchased a bulk license of seats, this field will display N/A.
    3. \n
  3. \nTotal Bulk Active Accounts: View the total number of your accounts that are using your bulk subscription.
    4. \n
  4. \nTotal Active Accounts: View the total number of your paid accounts.
    5. \n
  5. \nTotal Trial Accounts: View the total number of your trial accounts.
    6. \n
  6. \nTotal Free Accounts: View the total number of your free accounts.
    7. \n
  7. \nTotal Bulk Purchased Seats: View the total number of seats you've purchased for your bulk subscription. If you haven't purchased a bulk license of seats, this field will display N/A.
    8. \n
  8. \nTotal Bulk Active Users: View the total number of active users from your accounts that use your bulk subscription.
    9. \n
  9. \nTotal Active Users: View the total number of active users from your accounts with any paid subscription.\n
    \n
    \nNote:Users that were recently archived or restored may not be counted in your number of Total Active Users. This data typically refreshes overnight.
    \n
    \n
    10. \n
  10. \nNew Accounts by Month: View a graph that displays the number of new accounts in your console, grouped by the month they were created in.
    11. \n
\n
Personal Settings
\n
We recommend that you set up your console with the settings on your profile. To access your profile, click your email address on the top-right corner of the page, then click Personal Settings.
\n
Personal Details
\n
In your Personal Details subtab, you can update information such as your name, mobile phone number, multi-factor authorization (MFA) settings, and more. For more information, see the screenshot and list below:
\n
  
\n
    \n
  1. \nFirst Name: Enter your preferred first name.
    2. \n
  2. \nLast Name: Enter your preferred last name.
    3. \n
  3. \nTime Zone: Select your preferred time zone. Your selection will change how timestamps appear in the admin console.
    4. \n
  4. \nDate and Time Format: Select your preferred date and time format. Your selection will change how dates and times appear in the admin console.
    5. \n
  5. \nPhone Number: Enter your preferred phone number.
    6. \n
  6. \nExtension: Enter the extension of your preferred phone number, if applicable.
    7. \n
  7. \nMobile Phone Number: Enter your preferred mobile phone number.
    8. \n
  8. \nMulti-factor Authentication: Enable or disable multi-factor authentication (MFA) for your account. For more information, see our Partner and Multi-Account: Enable Two-Factor or Multi-factor Authentication on My Account article.
    9. \n
  9. \nChange Password: Click this button to change your password.
    10. \n
\n
After making any updates to this page, click Update Personal Details to save your changes.
\n
Notifications and Alerts
\n
You can use the Notifications and Alerts subtab to manage system notifications and alerts. Enable the Critical Alerts toggle to receive critical and time-sensitive notifications for platform updates that may require administrative attention or action. For more information, see our KnowBe4 Platform Notifications article.
\n
  
\n
Click Save Changes to confirm and save your updates.
\n
\n
\nTip:For your convenience, a Manage Notifications link is included at the bottom of every notification. Selecting the link opens a dedicated page where you can manage your preferences. For security purposes, access to this page expires after 24 hours. You can always manage your notification preferences by using the Notifications and Alerts subtab.
\n
\n
Account Settings
\n
To access your Account Settings, click your email address on the top-right corner of the page, and then click Account Settings. On this page, you can adjust your organization name, contact information, and the following settings:
\n
\n
    \n
  1. \nCompany Logo: You can change the logo displayed on the top-left corner of your account. Your logo will apply to all of your clients' accounts as well. The image you select must be in .png format and less than 300 KB.
    2. \n
  2. \nOnly Allow Console Sessions from One IP at a Time: You can enable this feature to prevent admins from having simultaneous console sessions on multiple IP addresses.
    3. \n
  3. \nRestrict Console Sessions to Specific IP Ranges: You can enable this option to restrict all account management admin sessions to the IP ranges entered below.
    4. \n
  4. \nAccount Session Timeout: You can select a time period of account inactivity from the drop-down menu. The system will automatically log you out after the specified period of inactivity. To change this option for your clients, it can be accessed under their Account Settings.
    5. \n
  5. \nSAML: You can enable SAML on your account for an added layer of security. For more information, see our SAML Integration Overview and Partner and Multi-Account: Enable SAML on Your Account Management Console articles.
    6. \n
  6. \nPurchased Add-ons: Your add-ons will appear here.
    7. \n
\n
Accounts
\n
You might need to view account details for your existing accounts or access these accounts as an admin. This can be done from the Accounts tab of your Account Management console. For details on how to manage existing accounts, see our Partner and Multi-Account: Viewing Your Accounts article.
\n
The different color backgrounds on the accounts indicate the type of account that they have. A yellow background color indicates a Free account, a dark gray background color indicates an Archived account, and an orange background color indicates a Trial account.
\n
\n
To add an account to your Account Management console, log in to the Partner Portal. Under the Contact Us drop-down menu, select Add Domain Account. You will need to provide the following information:
\n
    \n
  • Company Name (Legal Entity Name)
    • \n
  • Domain Name
    • \n
  • Street address, including Country, State or Province, and City
    • \n
  • The initial number of users that will be uploaded to the console
    • \n
  • Who will be managing the console?
    • \n
  • Do you have the client's permission to phish and train this domain?
    • \n
  • Whether the account will be a Paid account or Trial account
    • \n
\n
You will receive a response within 24 business hours.
\n
If you are adding a Trial account, the 30-day trial period begins the day the domain is added to your console.
\n
\n
\nNote: If you have users who frequently move between different managed accounts in your organization, please contact our support team to have the Modify email addresses of archived users synced to another KnowBe4 account option enabled for your account. For more information on this option, see our Partner and Multi-Account: Move Users Across Managed Accounts article.
\n
\n
Importing Users to Accounts
\n
You can bulk import all the users on a managed account at once by using our Import feature. For more information, see our Partner and Multi-Account: Import Users article.
\n
\n
Phishing
\n
From the Phishing tab, you can create and edit your managed phishing campaigns, managed email templates, and managed landing pages. See the subsections below for more information.
\n
Managed Phishing Campaigns
\n
You can create phishing campaigns for all of your managed accounts. For more information, see our Partner and Multi-Account: Create a Managed Phishing Campaign article.
\n
\n
You can also edit and monitor the phishing campaigns you have created. For more information, see our Partner and Multi-Account: Monitor a Managed Phishing Campaign article.
\n
Managing Email Templates
\n
You can create your own custom email templates for each of your managed accounts. For more information, see our Partner and Multi-Account: Monitor a Managed Phishing Campaign article.
\n
\n
You can also export email templates you have created and import them to other customer accounts. For more information, see our Partner and Multi-Account: Use the Import and Export Feature article.
\n
Managing Landing Pages
\n
You can create your own custom landing pages for each of your managed accounts. For more information, see our Partner and Multi-Account: Manage Landing Pages article.
\n
\n
You can also export landing pages you have created and import them to other customer accounts. For more information, see our Partner and Multi-Account: Use the Import and Export Feature article.
\n
Training
\n
From the Training tab, you can create and edit your managed training campaigns and managed training notification templates. See the subsections below for more information.
\n
Managing Training Campaigns
\n
You can use managed training campaigns to create training campaigns for multiple accounts from your Account Management console. For more information, see our Partner and Multi-Account: Create and Monitor Managed Training Campaigns article.
\n
\n
\nImportant: Before you can create a managed training campaign, you\u2019ll need to add content from the ModStore to your managed library. For more information, see the ModStore section below.
\n
\n
\n
Managing Notification Templates
\n
You can create custom training notifications for each of your managed accounts. For more information, see our Partner and Multi-Account: Create Managed Training Notifications article.
\n
\n
Partners
\n
From the Partners tab, you can view, edit, and delete your partners. To learn more about partners in your Account Management console, see our Partner and Multi-Account: Manage Partners article.
\n
\n
\nNote: You'll only see the Partners tab if this feature is enabled on your Account Management console. If you're interested in adding that feature, please contact your Account Manager.
\n
\n
\n
Admins
\n
From the Admins tab of your Account Management console, you can grant account management admin rights to additional users at your organization to assist you with managing your client accounts. Only admins with Full Access Admin permissions on the management account can grant additional administrator permissions to other users. To learn how to add another admin to your Account Management console, see our Partner and Multi-Account: Manage Partner Admins article.
\n
\n
ModStore
\n
From the ModStore tab of your Account Management console, you can view training content and add content to the libraries of your managed accounts. For more information, see our Partner and Multi-Account: Managed ModStore and Library Guide.
\n
\n
\nImportant: Before you can begin a managed training campaign, you must add content from the ModStore to your managed library.
\n
\n
\n
Reports
\n
You can view and create reports for any of your managed accounts. For more information, see our Partner and Multi-Account: Account Management Reporting Guide.
\n
