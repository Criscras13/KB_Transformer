Go to the Phishing\u00a0tab of your Account Management portal. Then, click the\u00a0Landing Pages\u00a0tab. From here you can view, edit, and categorize System Landing Pages and your own Managed Landing Pages. These landing pages will be available under\u00a0Phishing\u00a0>\u00a0Landing Pages\u00a0>\u00a0Managed Landing Pages\u00a0in each of your managed accounts.
\n
If you've never created a landing page before, we recommend starting by editing one of our built-in landing pages. First, go to the System Landing Pages tab under Landing Pages. Then, click\u00a0on the name of a landing page and use the editor to customize it to your liking. Once saved, this edited landing page will be added to your list of managed landing pages.
\n\n
You can also create your own custom landing page from scratch by clicking the + Create Landing Page\u00a0from the\u00a0Managed Landing Pages tab under Landing Pages.
\n\n
To access customized landing pages in your managed accounts, they must be added to a Managed Landing Page Category. You can categorize a landing page by clicking the checkbox next to the title, selecting a category from the options that appear at the top of the screen, and clicking Move.\u00a0
\n\n
For more details about how to customize landing pages, see our How to Create and Edit Email Templates and Landing Pages article or watch our Customization of Phishing Templates and Landing Pages video.
\n\n
Importing/exporting\u00a0
\n
In addition to the Managed Landing Pages tab, you can export landing pages you've created and import them to specific managed accounts. You can use this functionality if you want to add certain landing pages to only specific accounts or if one of your accounts makes a landing page you'd like to use in other accounts. For more information on how to use this, check out our article Importing and Exporting Phishing Templates and Landing Pages
