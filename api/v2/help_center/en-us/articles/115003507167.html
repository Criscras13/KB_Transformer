The below steps will allow you to configure single sign-on (SSO) with PortalGuard. This will enable your users to automatically log in to the KSAT console for their security awareness training.
\n
\n
\nNote:Make sure the email address that your users use to authenticate with SAML is either entered into the Email field or Email Aliases field of their user profile. However, only the email address listed in the Email field will receive training notification emails. For more information about adding information to user profiles, see our User Profile Guide.
\n
\n
To enable SAML/SSO on your account, please follow the instructions in our How to Set Up SAML Single Sign-on for the Security Awareness Training Platform article.
\n
