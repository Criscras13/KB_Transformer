Accessing KnowBe4
To log in to your KnowBe4 account, follow the steps below:
    \n
  1. Navigate to your KnowBe4 product login URL.
    2. \n
  2. Enter your email address and select Continue.
    3. \n
  3. Enter your email address and password.
    4. \n
  4. Select Login.
    5. \n
Login Page Features
The KnowBe4 login page includes the following elements:
    \n
  • \nSign-in fields: Enter your credentials to access your account
    • \n
  • \nProduct demos: Access links to schedule demonstrations for additional KnowBe4 products
    • \n
\nNote:Your login credentials remain the same as before the interface update. All training progress, certificates, and course materials are preserved during this transition.

Navigation Menu
The navigation menu provides centralized access to all KnowBe4 applications associated with your account from a single location.
\nNote:When you navigate to another product using the navigation menu, you may need to log in again for that product. If you are navigating from Defend or Prevent to KSAT or PhishER, you will need to select your region in the region selector drop-down menu on the login page.
Using the Navigation Menu
To switch between KnowBe4 products, follow the steps below:
    \n
  1. Log in to any KnowBe4 application.
    2. \n
  2. Locate the navigation menu in the top-left corner of your console.
    3. \n
  3. Select the product you want to access from the menu.
    4. \n
  4. You will be directed to the selected application.
    5. \n
