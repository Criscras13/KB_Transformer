Our Executive Reports feature allows you to create custom graphs and tables using widgets. You can use these graphs to display your organization\u2019s phishing, training, and Risk Score metrics.
\n
Sometimes, stakeholders may ask you to add specific information to your reports. In this article, you can find common metrics that stakeholders, such as security administrators, may want to view in your executive reports. For general information about the Executive Reports widgets, see our Executive Reports Overview article.
\n
Training Metrics
\n
Using the Training Metrics widget, you can create the following graphs and charts.
\n
Training Status by Manager
\n
You can create a table that displays the percentage of not started, complete, and in progress training for your managers\u2019 teams.
\n
To create this table, select the options listed in the table below:
