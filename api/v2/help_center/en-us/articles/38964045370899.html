Use this article to stay up-to-date with changes to KnowBe4's Artificial Intelligence Defense Agents.
\n
\n
\nNote: Changes may include new, updated, deprecated, or removed features. KnowBe4 reserves the right to terminate access to, use of, and support of features that are included as part of the Subscription Services, which is common with SaaS services as they are constantly changing and improving. This allows us to adapt to the evolving cybersecurity industry so we can provide current, high-quality services.
\n
\n
See the table below for a list of updates, with the latest updates displayed at the top.
\n
\n
\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n
Release DateDescription
October 2025\n
    \n
  • October 23, 2025 - We're excited to announce that AIDA Orchestration is now available as a technical preview. AIDA Orchestration represents the next evolution of our AI-driven security awareness platform, designed to automate and optimize your security awareness training intelligently. For more information, see the AIDA Orchestration Technical Preview article.
    • \n
\n
March 2025\n
    \n
  • March 27, 2025\u2014We've added the Recommended Landing Pages agent to AIDA. This agent automatically suggests a landing page with your AIDA-generated phishing templates, or you can manually select a preferred landing page when editing your templates. For more information, see the Viewing and Editing Your Phishing Template section of the AIDA Template Generation Guide.
    • \n
\n
February 2025\n
    \n
  • February 26, 2025 - We've made a few updates to the AIDA Training Report. You can now include archived user data in the report and apply new filters to your search results. In addition, you can view phishing failure details for each user, such as the reason for the assigned training and which phishing template the user failed. For more information, see the Reviewing AIDA Remedial Training Results section of the AIDA Automated Training Guide.
    • \n
  • February 11, 2025 - The ModStore page now includes a Knowledge Refresher filter under the Content Features drop-down menu. This filter is available only with an active AIDA add-on subscription.
    • \n
  • February 3, 2025 - The Training page now includes AIDA Knowledge Refreshers if the AIDA add-on is part of your subscription. AIDA Policy Quizzes are also compatible with the KnowBe4 Learner App with an active subscription. in addition, three new badges are available for Knowledge Refreshers and Policy Quizzes. We also fixed a problem where the time shown for finishing a module differed on the console and mobile app versions, and now both versions display the same time. For more information, see the AIDA Knowledge Refreshers Guide and Learner Support: Badges Guide.
    • \n
\n
January 2025\n
    \n
  • January 30, 2025 - We've added the AIDA Callback Phishing Agent, which allows you to leverage generative AI to create custom callback phishing templates on demand based on your organization's specifications. This AIDA agent gives you the ability to better control the difficulty of your template by simply selecting the difficulty level of the template or by, more specifically, choosing the individual Social Engineering Indicators (SEI) in the template. For more information, please see our AIDA Template Generation Guide.
    • \n

Release DateDescription
November 2024\n
    \n
  • November 22, 2024 - We've released KnowBe4's Artificial Intelligence Defense Agents (AIDA), which offer a powerful AI-driven solution for managing human risk in your organization. AIDA helps reduce risk and improve user behavior through four key agents: AI-generated phishing templates, automated training, policy quizzes, and knowledge refreshers. For more information about AIDA, please see our Get Started with AIDA article.
