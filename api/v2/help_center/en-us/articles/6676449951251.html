In this article, you will learn how to integrate Malwarebytes\u2019 endpoint protection (EPP) platform with SecurityCoach. Once the integration is complete, data provided by Malwarebytes will be available for use under the SecurityCoach tab of your KSAT console. This data can be viewed in SecurityCoach reports and used to create detection rules for real-time coaching campaigns. For general information about SecurityCoach, see our SecurityCoach Product Manual.
\n
\n\n\n
Create an OAuth Client
\n
Before you can set up this integration in your KSAT console, you will need to create an OAuth client in your Malwarebytes account.To create an OAuth client in your Malwarebytes account, follow the steps below:
\n
    \n
  1. Log in to your Malwarebytes account and navigate to Settings > APIs & Integrations. \n\n
    2. \n
  2. Click Add in the top-right corner of the page to open the Create Client window. \n\n
    3. \n
  3. Enter the Application name, and select the Read permission. \n\n
    4. \n
  4. Click Save to create your client and open the OAuth client window. The OAuth client window displays your Client ID and Client Secret. \n\n
    5. \n
  5. Contact Malwarebytes to learn how to obtain your Account ID.
    6. \n
  6. Copy and save your Client ID, Client Secret, and Account ID to somewhere that you can easily access. You will need these items when you complete the integration setup in the Set Up the Integration in Your KSAT Console section below.
    7. \n
\n\n\n
Set Up the Integration in Your KSAT Console
\n
Once you have created your Malwarebytes OAuth client, you can set up the integration in your KSAT console. To set up the integration, follow the steps below:
\n
    \n
  1. Log in to your KSAT console and navigate to SecurityCoach > Setup > Security Vendor Integrations.
    2. \n
  2. Locate Malwarebytes and click Configure.
    3. \n
  3. Enter the Client ID, Client Secret, and Account ID in the corresponding fields.\n\n
    4. \n
  4. Click Authorize.
    5. \n
\n\n\n
Map Your Users
\n
After you\u2019ve finished integrating Malwarebytes, you can map your users either through mapping rules (recommended) or through a CSV file upload. For more information about user mapping, see our Mapping Users in SecurityCoach article.
\n
Once you\u2019ve successfully authorized this integration, you can manage detection rules for Malwarebytes on the Detection Rules subtab of SecurityCoach. For a full list of available system detection rules for this vendor, see our Which Detection Rules Can I Use with My Vendors? article.
