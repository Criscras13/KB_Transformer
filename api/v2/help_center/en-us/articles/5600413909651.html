Team Dashboards are designed for managers to view information about their team, such as their team's training progress, phishing results, and Team Risk Score. If your organization has enabled Team Dashboards and you lead a team, you can view your Team Dashboard on the Dashboard tab of your Learner Experience.
\n
\n
\n
\nNote: The Team Dashboard is enabled and customized by your organization\u2019s administrators. Your Team Dashboard may not include all of the information listed in this article.
\n
\n
For more information about the Team Dashboard, click the tabs below:
\n
\n
\n
If any of your direct reports are managers, the Select a Team section will display on your dashboard. The Select a Team section allows you to choose which team you want to view on the Team Dashboard. In addition to your team, you can view the Team Dashboard for up to three levels of managers below you in your organization's management hierarchy. You will not be able to select an individual user from the Select a Team drop-down menu.
\n
For more information about selecting a team, see the screenshot and list below:
\n
\n
    \n
  1. \nSelect a team...: Use this drop-down list to select the manager whose team you want to view on the team dashboard.
    2. \n
  2. \nView: Click this button to load the team dashboard for the selected team.
    3. \n
  3. \nReset: Click this button to reset the team dashboard to your team.
    4. \n
\n
\n
\n
The Training Progress section displays the percentage of training assignments that your team has completed within the last year. This percentage does not include any optional learning content your team members have completed.
\n
\n
\n
\n
The Optional Learning section displays the percentage of team members who have completed optional learning content within the last year.
\n
\n
\n
\n
The Phishing Test Results section includes a summary of your team's PST results within the last year.
\n
For more information about each of the three possible results, see the list below:
\n
    \n
  • \nFailed: This result shows the number of times that your team members have failed a PST. Failures include clicking on links, opening attachments, enabling macros on attachments, or entering data on a landing page.
    • \n
  • \nReported: This result shows the number of times that your team members reported a PST using the Phish Alert Button (PAB).
    • \n
  • \nNot Reported: This result shows the number of times that your team members received a PST but did not fail the PST or report it.
    • \n
\n
This section also includes your team\u2019s combined Phish-prone Percentage. For more information about Phish-prone Percentage, see our How Is Phish-prone Percentage Calculated? article.
\n
\n
\n
\n
The Team Risk Score section displays your team\u2019s current Team Risk Score. This score is based on factors such as your team's PST results and completed training. For more information, see our Learner Support: SmartRisk Agent\u2122 and Risk Score article.
\n
\n
\n
\n
The Team Details for the Last Year section displays information about all users in the team you are viewing. This information includes the number of team members who have overdue training, have enrolled themselves in optional learning content, and failed a Phishing Security Test (PST). Depending on your organization\u2019s preferences, you may also see a list of your team members.
\n
You can use the subtabs in this section to filter your team\u2019s details. For more information see the screenshot and list below:
\n
\n
    \n
  1. \nTraining Overdue: This subtab includes each team member with training assignments that are past the due date but can still be completed.
    2. \n
  2. \nTraining Due Soon: This subtab includes each team member with training assignments due in the next seven days.
    3. \n
  3. \nCompleted Training: This subtab includes each team member with all of their assigned training completed in the past year.
    4. \n
  4. \nOptional Learning: This subtab includes each team member with added optional learning content from their library in the past year.
    5. \n
  5. \nPhishing Failures: This subtab includes each team member along with their total number of phishing failures and reported PSTs in the past year.\n
    \n
    \nImportant:We display one phishing failure per PST, even if the user has multiple failures.
    \n
    \n
    6. \n
  6. \nUser Table: This table shows a list of all team members. Depending on your organization\u2019s account settings, the table will display the user\u2019s name, email address, total phishing failures, total phishing emails reported, and overall progress on assigned training campaigns.\n
    \n
    \nImportant: The Training Progress bar does not track optional learning.
    \n
    \n
    7. \n
  7. \nUser Information: This section displays additional information for each user depending on which subtab you have selected. For example, if you have the Phishing Failures subtab selected, you will see additional phishing information about the user. To access the information, click anywhere on the specific user\u2019s row.
    8. \n
  8. \nView or Hide All: Clicking View All will display a list of all users in the team you are viewing, including those that wouldn\u2019t normally fall under that subtab. Clicking Hide All will revert you back to the standard subtab view.
    9. \n
