Hiding a phishing campaign can be useful for any test campaigns you have running to avoid skewing your Phish-prone Percentage, test results, and Risk Score.
\n
You can either hide a phishing campaign from your reports when you're creating the campaign or any time after the campaign has been created. See the sections below for details.
\n
Hide Phishing Campaign During Creation
\n
Follow the steps below to hide a phishing campaign during its creation:
\n
    \n
  1. From the KSAT console, navigate to the Phishing tab.
    2. \n
  2. Click the +\u00a0Create Phishing Campaign button.
    3. \n
  3. During your campaign setup, select the Hide from Reports check box.
    4. \n
\n
\n
Hide Phishing Campaign After Creation
\n
Follow the steps below to hide a phishing campaign after its creation:
\n
    \n
  1. From the KSAT console, navigate to Phishing tab, then click the\u00a0Campaigns tab.
    2. \n
  2. Click the Actions drop-down menu to the right of the phishing campaign you'd like to hide.
    3. \n
  3. Select Hide from Reports. \n
    4. \n
  4. Once prompted, click Confirm to hide your phishing campaign from reports. \n
    5. \n
\n
You can also unhide the campaign if you change your mind in the future by following the same steps by clicking Show in Reports instead on a hidden campaign. Hidden campaigns will have a Hidden from Reports label and can also be found in the Hidden subtab for phishing campaigns.\u00a0
