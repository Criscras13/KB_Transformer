If you're reading this, it's likely that your organization is using the KnowBe4 Learner Experience (LX) to assign training content. You can customize your LX by selecting a preferred language, and you may also be able to earn badges and compete against your peers.
\n
\n
\nNote:If you are looking for information about the KnowBe4 Learner App, visit our Learner Support: KnowBe4 Learner App article.
\n
\n
Read below to learn more about these features.
\n
Localization
\n
You can set your preferred Learner Experience (LX) language by clicking your name in the top-right corner of your account and then clicking the desired language at the bottom of the drop-down menu. The interface for the LX will be translated to the selected language and courses that are available in that language that have not been started will default to that language.
\n
For the full list of supported languages, visit our Multi-Language Support and Localization page.
\n
\n
Dashboard
\n
The Dashboard tab displays an overview of your security awareness training, such as your training progress, phishing test results, badges, and more. If you have team members who report directly to you, you may also see a Team Dashboard. To learn more about the Team Dashboard, visit our Learner Support: Team Dashboard Overview for Managers article.
\n
\n
\n
\nNote:The Dashboard tab is enabled and customized by your organization\u2019s administrators. If enabled, your dashboard may not include all of the tiles and information that is listed in this article.
\n
\n
Click on the tabs below to learn more about each section of your dashboard.
\n
\n
\n
The Training Progress tile shows your percentage of completed training assignments and includes a link to your training.
\n
\n
The assignments tile displays your five most recent training assignments, along with the training status and when the training is due. You can also click on a row to be taken to that specific training assignment.
\n
\n
\n
\n
The messages tile indicates how many unread messages you have.
\n
\n
You can click View Messages to go to the Messages tab.
\n
\n
\n
The Phishing Test Results tile shows the number of reported phishing emails and phishing failures you have had.
\n
\n
The Reported Phishing Emails bar shows the number of simulated phishing emails you have reported, without also failing the test.
\n
The Phishing Failures bar shows the number of negative interactions you have had with simulated phishing emails. Actions such as replying, downloading attachments, and clicking a link within the email are considered phishing failures.
\n
\n
\n
The Personal Risk Score tile shows your calculated risk level. For more information about your Personal Risk Score and how to improve it, visit our Learner Support: SmartRisk Agent\u2122 and Risk Score article.
\n
\n
\n
\n
The badges tile shows how many badges you have earned and includes a link to our Learner Support: How to Earn Badges article.
\n
\n
The leaderboard tile shows where your group ranks against other groups. It also includes a link to view the leaderboard.
\n
\n
\n
\n
Training
\n
From the Training tab, you can see all of your assignments. If you are enrolled in multiple training assignments, your assignments will be displayed by priority. Any assignments that are past due will be shown first since they are the highest priority. Any incomplete assignments that will expire within the next 24 hours will also appear toward the top of your training screen.
\n
To learn how to begin your training or how to resume a training course, please see step 4 of our Learner Support: A 4-Step Guide to Getting Started article.
\n
\n
\nRemember:For training progress to be accurately reflected in the system, it is crucial to close out the training window properly upon completion. This action ensures the completion status is updated in the console. Learners should be reminded that they must reach the last slide and select any Complete Training or similar buttons, and avoid selecting the browser's Close button before official completion.
\n
\n
Library
\n
On the Library tab, you will find optional learning content that you can complete whenever you would like. Depending on the settings set by your organization\u2019s administrators, you will either see a preselected library or a full library. Read the corresponding section below for more information about your Library tab.
\n
Preselected Library
\n
The content listed in a preselected library has been selected by your organization. We encourage you to browse these options and find content that interests you.
\n
\n
When you find a piece of content that you\u2019d like to complete, follow the steps below:
\n
    \n
  1. Click on the content\u2019s image or the View Details button.
    2. \n
  2. On the page that opens, review the content description and the estimated time you will need to complete the content.
    3. \n
  3. If you would like to begin this optional learning content, click the Start button.
    4. \n
\n
If you start a piece of content but do not complete it, you\u2019ll see the In Progress tag on the content\u2019s image. Once you complete a piece of content, you\u2019ll see the Completed tag. You can hover your mouse over the completed content\u2019s image to see your time spent, completion date, and a link to download your completion certificate.
\n
\n
Full Library
\n
Content listed in a full library is organized into different types of recommendations and includes a search feature.
\n
\n
For more information about the different types of recommendations, see the list below:
\n
    \n
  • \nRecommended For You: The content listed in this section has been selected by KnowBe4\u2019s Artificial Intelligence Driven Agent (AIDA). AIDA recommends content based on the following information:\n
      \n
    • Optional content that you have completed.
      • \n
    • Required content that you have completed on the Training tab.
      • \n
    • How you have rated your completed required content.As you complete more content and provide more feedback, you will receive more relevant recommendations.
      • \n
    \n
    • \n
\n
    \n
  • \nRecommended by Your Organization: You will only see this section if your admins have enabled it. The content listed in this section is optional learning that has been selected by your organization.
    • \n
  • \nRecommended Topic: Social Media: The content listed in this section covers social media, which is one of our most popular and relevant topics.
    • \n
\n
At the top of the Library tab, you can browse your recommended content by using the Content Types and Topics drop-down menus or by searching for keywords. If you have set your training language, content available in that language will be shown first in your recommendations and search results.
\n
When you find a piece of content that you like, follow the steps below:
\n
    \n
  1. Click on the content\u2019s image or the View Details button.
    2. \n
  2. On the page that opens, review the content description and the estimated time you will need to complete the content.
    3. \n
  3. If you would like to complete this piece of content, click the Enroll button.
    4. \n
  4. From the modal that opens, click the Start Now button to begin the content now. Or, click the Save for Later button to complete the content at a later time.\n
      \n
    • Content that you\u2019re enrolled in but haven\u2019t started can be found in the Continue Learning section of your library.
      • \n
    \n
    5. \n
\n
If you start a piece of content but do not complete it, you\u2019ll see the In Progress tag on the content\u2019s image. This content will be listed in the Continue Learning section of your library.
\n
\n
Once you complete a piece of content, you\u2019ll see a Completed tag on the content\u2019s image. You can hover your mouse over the completed content\u2019s image to see your time spent, completion date, and a link to download your completion certificate.
\n
\n
Messages
\n
The Messages tab lists messages sent to you from your organization. If you have unread messages, you will see a number next to this tab.
\n
\n
Once you have read a message, click Mark as Read to move the message to the Read Messages section at the bottom of the page. You can review any message in the Read Messages section by clicking on the message subject.
\n
Leaderboard
\n
The Leaderboard tab shows where you or your group rank in a friendly competition with your colleagues. For more information about leaderboards and how they work, see our Learner Support: Leaderboard Guide.
\n
\n
Badges
\n
As you complete your training, you will have the opportunity to earn badges. You'll earn them automatically based on various achievements. You can click the Badges tab to see what badges you've received and what badges are still available to you.
\n
\n
To learn more information about how to earn badges, see our Learner Support: How to Earn Badges article.
\n
User Profile
\n
You can access your user profile by clicking on your name in the top-right corner of your account, and then clicking on Profile from the drop-down menu. In your profile, you can update information such as your name, phone number, and password.
\n
\n
Once you have made your updates, click the  Save button to save the changes. You will be taken back to the LX home screen.
\n
If you experience any problems with the LX please contact our support team.
