When your organization undergoes an audit, the auditor may need to verify that a sample of evidence was in effect during a specific time frame. For example, it may be required that your employees take compliance training every year. This means the compliance training would only be applicable for one year. To record this time frame in your KCM GRC platform, users can enter an Applicable Date Range for the evidence that they submit for control tasks.\u00a0
\n
\n
\nNote:This feature is currently being tested by select customers, but it will be widely available soon. If you would like to be part of our early access testing, please contact your Customer Success Manager.
\n
\n
The Applicable Date Range feature is available to accounts that have enabled Effective Date Range (EDR) for task schedules. To check if your account has EDR enabled, navigate to your Account Settings and see the Allow Effective Date Range in task schedules field.
\n
This guide explains how to submit an applicable date range for evidence and it explains the areas of your platform where you can view the evidence's applicable date range.
\n
How Do I Add an Applicable Date Range to Evidence?
\n
You will apply an Applicable Date Range (ADR) to your evidence from the View Task page. You can apply an ADR when you are adding new evidence or when you are updating existing evidence.\u00a0
\n
\n
\nNote:To see the full instructions for submitting evidence, see our How to Monitor and Complete Tasks article.
\n
\n
The Applicable Date Range field is found at the bottom of the Create Link, Update Link, Upload Document, or Update Document window. Follow the steps below to add an ADR:
\n
    \n
  1. Click the Applicable Date Range field to open the date picker window that you will use to select your date range.\n\n
    2. \n
  2. Click the beginning date in your applicable date range. Use the arrows at the top of the calendar to scroll through the months.\n
    \n
    \nTip:You can click on the name of the month and year at the top of the calendar to quickly navigate to the month and year that you are looking for.
    \n
    \n
    3. \n
  3. Hover over the ending date in your applicable date range. Before clicking, you can look at the Applicable Date Range field to ensure you are selecting the desired date range, as shown below.\n\n
    4. \n
  4. Click the end date for your time frame to close the date picker.
    5. \n
  5. Click the Update button at the bottom right-hand corner of the window. This button may have a different name depending on which window you are adding the ADR to.\u00a0\n\n
    6. \n
\n
