In this article, you\u2019ll learn how to integrate your Noetic Cyber account with your KnowBe4 console. This integration allows you to pull your phishing and training data into Noetic Cyber\u2019s Security Asset Management platform.
\n
\n
\nNote: Before you set up this integration, you'll need to copy the Reporting API Token from your KnowBe4 console Account Settings. For more information, see the API section of our KnowBe4 Console Account Settings: Account Integrations article.
\n
\n
To learn how to integrate your KnowBe4 console with your Noetic Cyber account, follow the steps below.
\n
    \n
  1. Log in to your Noetic Cyber account.
    2. \n
  2. From the menu on the left side of the page, select the Connectors icon.
    3. \n
  3. On the Connectors page, click the KnowBe4 tile.\n\n
    4. \n
  4. In the KnowBe4 window, click the Settings tab.
    5. \n
  5. In the Global section, paste your Reporting API token in the KnowBe4 API Key field.\n\n
    6. \n
  6. Click Save.
    7. \n
\n
After the integration is complete, you\u2019ll see a success message confirming the connection.
