All admins and Security Role users in your KSAT console have access to their own Personal Settings. The Personal Settings page is used to update personal information and preferences such as name, time zone, preferred date and time format, phone number, password, and Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) settings. For more information about how to set up MFA for your account, see our Enable Two-Factor or Multi-Factor Authentication on Your Account article.
\n
\n
\nNote: If you're an end-user, read the User Profile section of our Learner Support: Navigating the Learner Experience (LX) article or watch our Editing Your Profile video for information on how to access your profile.
\n
\n
Accessing Your Profile
\n
To access your Personal Settings, follow the steps below:
\n
    \n
  1. Log in to your KSAT console.
    2. \n
  2. Click on your email address in the top-right corner of the page.\n
      
    \n
    3. \n
  3. From the drop-down menu, click Personal Settings.\n
    \n
    4. \n
\n
Personal Details
\n
In your Personal Details subtab, you can update information such as your name, mobile phone number, MFA settings, and more. For more information, see the screenshot and list below:
\n
  
\n
    \n
  1. \nFirst Name: Enter your preferred first name.
    2. \n
  2. \nLast Name: Enter your preferred last name.
    3. \n
  3. \nTime Zone: Select your preferred time zone. Your selection will change how timestamps appear to you in the admin console.
    4. \n
  4. \nDate and time format: Select your preferred date and time format. Your selection will change how dates and times appear to you in the admin console.
    5. \n
  5. \nPhone Number: Enter your preferred phone number.
    6. \n
  6. \nExtension: Enter the extension of your preferred phone number, if applicable.
    7. \n
  7. \nMobile Phone Number: Enter your preferred mobile phone number.
    8. \n
  8. \nMulti-factor Authentication: Enable or disable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for your account. For more information, see our Enable Two-Factor or Multi-Factor Authentication on Your Account article.
    9. \n
  9. \nChange Password: Click this button to change your password.
    10. \n
  10. \nLog Out of All Sessions: Click this button to log yourself out of all active sessions in the KSAT console.
    11. \n
\n
After making any updates to this page, click Update Profile to save your changes.
\n
Notifications and Alerts
\n
You can use the Notifications and Alerts subtab to manage system notifications and alerts. For more information, see the screenshot and list below:
\n
\n
\nNote: The SecurityCoach Notification Subscriptions section is only available for customers with a SecurityCoach subscription.
\n
\n
  
\n
    \n
  1. \nPlatform Critical Alerts: Receive critical and time-sensitive notifications for platform updates that may require administrative attention or action. For more information, see our KnowBe4 Platform Notifications article.
    2. \n
  2. \nSecurityCoach Critical Alerts: Receive alerts and regular reminders when critical SecurityCoach updates are required or a vendor integration fails.\n
    \n
    \nImportant:Critical alerts are always sent in real-time, and reminders about unresolved SecurityCoach critical alerts are sent every three calendar days.
    \n
    \n
    3. \n
  3. \nIntegration Health: Receive alerts and recommendations for your vendor integrations.
    4. \n
  4. \nUser Management: Receive alerts and recommendations for mapping your users to identifiers and risky activity.
    5. \n
  5. \nSystem Detection Rules: Receive alerts about updates to system detection rules.
    6. \n
  6. \nCampaign Insights: Receive summaries and recommendations for your real-time coaching campaigns.
    7. \n
  7. \nNotification Frequency: Each category can be customized to send at different regular intervals:\n
      \n
    • \nDaily: Receive the selected notification every business day at 2:00 p.m. in your organization\u2019s set time zone.\n
      \n
      \nNote:If 2:00 p.m. is not in your organization\u2019s set business hours, the notification will be sent at the next available business hour. To update your organization\u2019s business days and hours, see our KnowBe4 Console Account Settings: Account Information article.
      \n
      \n
      • \n
    • \nWeekly: Receive the selected notification every Wednesday at 2:00 p.m. in your organization\u2019s set time zone.\n
      \n
      \nNote:If Wednesday at 2:00 p.m. is not in your organization\u2019s set business hours, the notification will be sent at the next available business hour. To update your organization\u2019s business days and hours, see our KnowBe4 Console Account Settings: Account Information article.
      \n
      \n
      • \n
    \n
    8. \n
  8. \nNotification Delivery Method: Select how you would like to receive notifications and alerts. If a problem occurs with a messaging app, notifications will be sent by email.\n
    \n
    \nNote:To receive notifications using messaging apps, you must first configure the messaging app in your SecurityCoach console. For more information, see our Configuring Delivery Methods articles.
    \n
    \n
    9. \n
\n
Click\u00a0Save Changes to confirm and save your updates.
\n
\n
\nTip:For your convenience, a Manage Notifications link is included at the bottom of every notification. Selecting the link opens a dedicated page where you can manage your preferences. For security purposes, access to this page expires after 24 hours. You can always manage your notification preferences by using the Notifications and Alerts subtab
