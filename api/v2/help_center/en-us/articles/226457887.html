In Account Settings, you can view your account\u2019s subscription information, enable or disable specific features, and customize your KSAT console and Learner Experience (LX). To access your Account Settings, log in to your KSAT console and click your email address in the top-right corner of the page. Then, click Account Settings.
\n
To navigate to the different sections of your Account Settings, click the section names on the left side of the page. To expand a section, click the arrow icon next to the section name or click Expand All to expand all sections. You can also use the search bar at the top-right corner of the page to search for keywords in your Account Settings.
\n
\n
After you update any settings, be sure to click the blue Save Changes button at the bottom of the page.
\n
To learn more about the settings available in each section, see the list below:
\n
    \n
  • \nAccount Information Settings: In the Account Information section, you can review your subscription details, update your privacy settings, update your organization\u2019s information, edit placeholders, and more. For more information, see our KnowBe4 Console Account Settings: Account Information article.
    • \n
  • \nUser Management Settings: In the User Provisioning section, you can edit your user provisioning settings. You have the ability to use Active Directory Integration (ADI) or SCIM to provision your users. For more information, see our KnowBe4 Console Account Settings: User Management article.
    • \n
  • \nPhishing Settings: In the Phishing section, you can update your phishing campaign settings and enable Direct Message Injection (DMI). For more information, see our KnowBe4 Console Account Settings: Phishing article.
    • \n
  • \nTraining Settings: In the Training section, you can update your training and Learner Experience (LX) settings. For more information, see our KnowBe4 Console Account Settings: Training article.
    • \n
  • \nAccount Integrations Settings: In the Account Integrations section, you can manage your account integrations, including SAML, the Phish Alert Button (PAB), API reporting, PhishER, and, if you have a Diamond-level subscription, Second Chance. For more information, see our KnowBe4 Console Account Settings: Account Integrations article.
    • \n
  • \nReports Settings: In the Reports section, you can manage your reporting settings. For more information, see our KnowBe4 Console Account Settings: Reports article.
    • \n
