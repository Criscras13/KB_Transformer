This article shows admins how to customize the Phish Alert Button (PAB) display settings and provides visual examples of how these customizations will appear across different email platforms. By configuring these display options, admins can tailor the PAB's appearance, messaging, and user feedback to match their organization's requirements and branding standards across all supported email environments.
If you'd like additional information about the PAB, see our Phish Alert Button (PAB) Product Manual.
