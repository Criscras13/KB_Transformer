As you complete your training or report phishing emails, you can earn badges. Each badge is based on a specific achievement, such as completing multiple training assignments within 24 hours or reporting 100 simulated phishing emails. On the Badges tab of your Learner Experience (LX), you can view all the badges you\u2019ve already earned as well as any available badges.
\n
\n
\nImportant:You can only earn badges allowed by your organization. If your organization adds a new badge and you complete the achievement before the badge is added, you will not earn the badge.
\n
\n
