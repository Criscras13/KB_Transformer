We constantly add and update ModStore content to help keep your users informed about important security topics. To run a training campaign that includes the newest content, you will need to add the training content to your library.
\n
To add this new content to your library, follow the steps below:
\n
    \n
  1. Log in to your KSAT console and navigate to the ModStore tab.
    2. \n
  2. Open the Additional Filters menu by clicking on the plus sign under the Search box.
    3. \n
  3. From the Subscription Levels drop-down menu, select My Subscriptions. When you apply this filter, you will only see content that is available for your subscription level.
    4. \n
  4. From the Sort By drop-down menu, select Most Recent to view the newest content releases.\n\n
    5. \n
  5. To learn more about a piece of content, you can click the card or click the View Details button.
    6. \n
  6. To add content to your library, click the + Add to Library button on the content\u2019s card or Item Details page.\n\n
    7. \n
\n
Once added, the piece of content will be listed on the Library subtab and can be included in training campaigns.
\n\n
For more information about training content, see our ModStore and Library Guide. To learn how to create a campaign with new ModStore content, see our Creating and Managing Training Campaigns guide.
