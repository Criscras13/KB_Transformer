Whenever you have tasks to complete, tasks to approve, or other items to attend to in your KCM GRC platform, the system will automatically send you email notification reminders. In addition to individual notifications, if you are scheduled to receive more than one notification around the same time, the KCM GRC Email Digest combines your notifications into one email. This digest makes it so you'll receive a smaller number of notifications from your platform without missing important updates.
\n
KCM GRC's email notifications will either have a subject line that is specific to the purpose of the email, or the subject line will state \"KCM GRC Email Digest\". All emails with the \"KCM GRC Email Digest\" subject line contain more than one notification.
\n
All emails that are generated from KCM GRC are sent from the kcm[at]knowbe4.com email address.\u00a0
\n
When Will I Receive an Email Digest Instead of Individual Emails?
\n
Whenever you are set to receive two or more notifications within five minutes of each other, the notifications will be combined into one email digest.
\n
This means, every time an email is triggered for you, KCM GRC queues the email for approximately five minutes and waits to see if you will receive an additional notification. Once this five-minute time span has passed and no additional emails have been triggered, your individual notification or email digest will be sent.\u00a0
\n
The following list explains some of the most common scenarios where you would receive an email digest instead of individual email notifications:\u00a0
\n
    \n
  1. You are assigned to a task as the User Responsible and you have multiple tasks that are due soon or are past due around the same time period.\n
    \n
    \nImportant: An email digest can contain notifications for tasks that are due soon and tasks that are past due.\u00a0
    \n
    \n
      \n
    • Every day at 8 AM EST (12 PM UTC), the system checks for and sends task reminder notifications to the User Assigned.\u00a0
      • \n
    • To learn more about KCM GRC's task reminder notifications, see the Control Task Notifications section below.\u00a0
      • \n
    \n
    2. \n
  2. You are assigned to a task as the Approving Manager or Second-level Approving Manager, or if you are the group lead for a control, and you have multiple tasks that are awaiting your approval.\u00a0\n
      \n
    • Every day at 8 AM EST (12 PM UTC), the system checks for and sends reminder notifications to the Approving Manager, Second-level Approving Manager, or Group Lead if they have outstanding task approvals.
      • \n
    \n
    3. \n
  3. Your administrator is creating new task schedules or one-time tasks for controls and they've assigned you as the User Responsible for these tasks.\n
      \n
    • You are notified whenever you are assigned to a new one-time task or task schedule for a control. If your administrator assigns you to a task, the system waits approximately five minutes before sending a notification. If your administrator assigns you to additional tasks in this five-minute time span, the notifications will be combined into one email digest.
      • \n
    \n
    \n
    \nNote: For task schedules that use Effective Date Range, instead of notifying the User Assigned when the task schedule is created, the \"New Task Assigned\" email is sent on the Start Date of the first task in the schedule.
    \n
    \n
    4. \n
\n
What Types of Notifications Are Not Included in the Email Digest?
\n
The following types of emails are never included in an email digest:
\n
    \n
  • Emails that are sent for the Executive Reports that you have set up.\u00a0\n
      \n
    • To learn more, see our Executive Reporting article.\u00a0
      • \n
    \n
    • \n
  • Emails that are sent to users that allow them to activate their new user account.\u00a0\n
      \n
    • To learn more, see our How to Activate Your Account, Access Your Account, and Reset Your Password\u00a0article.\u00a0
      • \n
    \n
    • \n
  • Emails that are sent to users when they've forgotten their password.\u00a0\n
      \n
    • To learn more, see our How to Activate Your Account, Access Your Account, and Reset Your Password\u00a0article.\u00a0
      • \n
    \n
    • \n
  • Emails to notify you that the CSV Export you've created is ready for download.\n
      \n
    • To learn more, see our Data Exports Guide.\u00a0
      • \n
    \n
    • \n
\n
All other notifications are eligible for the KCM GRC Email Digest.
\n

\n
When you create a task schedule or a one-time task for a control, the user who is assigned to the task (User Assigned) will automatically receive notifications from KCM GRC.
\n
If you assign an Approving Manager or a Second-Level Approving Manager to a task schedule or a one-time task, they will also receive reminder notifications as necessary.
\n
If you've assigned a group to a control (under our user groups workflow), the Group Lead will receive reminder notifications for the control and its tasks, as necessary.\u00a0

Vendor Questionnaire Notifications
\n
Under the Vendor Risk Management module, you can send questionnaires to your vendors, or other third parties, to assess the level of risk involved in working with this entity. After the vendor has completed your questionnaire, you can create an \"issue\" in order to further discuss your concerns with an undesirable response to a question. To learn more about the Vendor Risk Management module, please see our Introduction Guide.
\n
Many of the workflows under the Vendor Risk Management module are carried out through the vendor profiles that you've added to your account. For example, you will send and review questionnaires from this area. When creating a vendor profile, you assign a Vendor Contact and a Vendor Owner:
\n
    \n
  • The Vendor Contact is the person who will be responding to your questionnaires on behalf of the third-party organization.
    • \n
  • The Vendor Owner is the KCM GRC user from your organization who will manage the questionnaires for that vendor.\u00a0
    • \n

Policy Campaign Notifications
\n
Under the Policy Management module, you will create policy campaigns for the various policies and agreements that are required of the employees in your organization. Your users will receive and acknowledge the policy agreements by email. When your users acknowledge the policies, the acknowledgments are recorded in your KCM GRC platform.\u00a0
\n
When creating a policy campaign, you will set up automatic email notifications for the users in your campaign so they can read and acknowledge policies. To learn about these email notifications, please see the User Experience section of our Policy Management Module Guide.
\n
\n
\nNote: If a user is enrolled in two or more policy campaigns at one time, they may receive multiple policy acknowledgment notifications in one email digest. To learn more about the email digest, see the KCM GRC Email Digest section above.
\n
\n
Account Activation Notifications
\n
Whenever you add a new user to KCM GRC, they will immediately receive an email notification to activate their account. Additionally, if you enable a user that was previously disabled, they will receive an email to activate their account. To learn more about this email and account activations, please see our How to Activate Your Account, Access Your Account, and Reset Your Password\u00a0article.\u00a0
\n
CSV Export Notifications
\n
Whenever you generate a CSV export from an area of your account, you will download the export from the Data Exports page. Depending on the amount of data included in your export, some CSV files may take more time to generate. Once your CSV file is ready for download, you will receive an email notification from KCM GRC. To learn more, please see our Data Exports Guide.\u00a0
