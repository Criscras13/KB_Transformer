Our training campaign notifications can be used to notify users, managers, and KSAT console admins of a user's enrollment in or progress on a training campaign.
\n
We have two main categories of notifications: automated and manual. Automated notifications are set up when creating or editing your campaign details. Manual notifications can be sent at any time during your users' enrollment period, from under your training campaign's overview page or reports page.
\n
Select the links below to learn more about training notifications, including detailed explanations for each notification type. Alternatively, you can watch our Automated Training Notifications and our Manual Training Notifications videos.
\n
\n
\nNote: When notifications are enabled for large training campaigns, KnowBe4's system automatically staggers email delivery. This approach helps protect email servers, improve deliverability, avoid spam filters, and maintain system performance. This method is particularly useful for navigating greylisting settings, which might block multiple simultaneous connections from the same IP addresses.
\n
\n
Automated Notifications
\n
You can set up automated notifications while creating or editing a new training campaign.
\n
To set up automated notifications when you create a campaign, select the + Add Notification button on the Create New Training Campaign page. You can also add automated notifications when you edit an existing campaign from the Edit Training Campaign page.
\n
\n
You can edit or add automated notifications for a campaign already in progress, but these notifications will not be sent retroactively. Alternatively, you could use a manual training notification to send a new notification for a campaign in progress.
\n
For example, if you have users who miss their training deadline and you want to allow them to complete the training after it's due, you can add Past Due notifications to your campaign. See our How to Extend Training Deadlines article to learn more about extending training for users who have missed their due date.
\n
Automated notifications are scheduled in relation to the start and end times of the training campaign. For best results, we recommend keeping the start and end times within the business hours set in your KSAT Account Settings.
\n

Best Practices for Automated Past Due Notifications
\n
You can schedule Past Due notifications to be sent continuously until the training assignments are complete. From the Create New Training Campaign or Edit Training Campaign page, use the + Add Notification button to schedule automated Past Due notifications for users, managers, or admins.
\n
We recommend using our manual training notifications even though automated training notifications can be modified after a campaign is in progress. Automated training notifications do not work retroactively.
\n
