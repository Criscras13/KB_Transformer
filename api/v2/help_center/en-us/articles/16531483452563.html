In this article, you\u2019ll learn how to get started with KnowBe4 if your organization uses Microsoft 365. This article will cover how to whitelist our mail servers, add our Phish Alert Button (PAB), enable single sign-on (SSO) for your users, set up user provisioning, and integrate our SecurityCoach product.
\n
For more information on connecting your Microsoft 365 account to your KSAT console, read the sections below.
