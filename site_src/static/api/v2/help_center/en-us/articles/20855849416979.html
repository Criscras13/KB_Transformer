By default, your KnowBe4 Security Awareness Training (KSAT) console is automatically integrated with your SecurityCoach product. This integration allows you to use phishing data from KSAT to create real-time coaching campaigns in SecurityCoach.
\n
To view your integration, log in to your KSAT console and navigate to SecurityCoach > Setup > Security Vendor Integrations. Under the Active Integrations section, you\u2019ll see the KSAT vendor tile.
\n
Click Edit on the KSAT vendor tile to view the detection rules for this integration or delete the integration.\u00a0
\n
\n
To review your KSAT detection rules and use them in a real-time coaching campaign, click Review Detection Rules. For more information, see our Real-Time Coaching Campaigns Guide.
\n
\n
To delete this integration, click Delete Integration.\u00a0
\n
