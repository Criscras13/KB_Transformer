\n

In your KSAT console, you\u2019ll find two new landing pages and four new phishing templates, which have been localized into all of our supported languages. These resources relate to the topic covered in each week of the Weekly Planner. We recommend using these landing pages and phishing templates together for a cohesive and engaging learning experience. To increase participation with your users, we recommend enabling the Leaderboard. For more information, see the Learner Experience (LX) section of our KnowBe4 Console Account Settings: Training article.

Cybersecurity Awareness Month Landing Pages and Phishing Templates
\n
For Cybersecurity Awareness Month, we created four phishing templates and two new landing pages. Each phishing template is an example of a common cybersecurity threat, such as social engineering and malicious URLs. Each landing page teaches users how to protect themselves and your organization from these threats.
