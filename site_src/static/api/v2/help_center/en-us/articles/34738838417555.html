In this article, you'll learn about the Reports section of your KSAT account settings. In the Reports section, you can update settings related to reporting.
\n
Date Format on CSVs
\n
You can select the Enable UTC Format on CSVs option in the Reports section of our KnowBe4 Account Settings and Partner Account Settings. This setting formats CSV reports to download in Coordinated Universal Time (UTC). It only affects reports that include date range options.
\n
\n
\nNote:CSV downloads from the Reports tab will always be in UTC.
