The Health Check feature allows admins to verify that Defend is properly configured and functioning as expected. It runs a series of tests on both inbound and outbound email processing.
\n
Tests Performed
\n
The health check runs the following tests:
\n
    \n
  • Inbound Email Tests\n
      \n
    • Confirmation of test email receipt
      • \n
    • Verification of Defend banner presence
      • \n
    • Check for the absence of multiple disclaimers
      • \n
    • Check for the absence of multiple subject phrases
      • \n
    • Verification of email authentication
      • \n
    \n
    • \n
  • Outbound Email Tests\n
      \n
    • Confirmation of email forwarding\u00a0
      • \n
    • Verification of banner removal\u00a0
      • \n
    • Check for the absence of duplicate disclaimers\u00a0
      • \n
    • Check for the absence of duplicate subject phrases
      • \n
    \n
    • \n
\n
How to Run a Health Check\u00a0
\n
\n
\nWarning:Only run one health check at a time.\u00a0
\n
\n
To run a health check, follow the steps below:
\n
    \n
  1. Log in to the Defend admin console.
    2. \n
  2. Navigate to\u00a0Settings\u00a0> Health Check.
    3. \n
  3. Select\u00a0Run Health Check.\u00a0
    4. \n
  4. Enter an internal test user email address.\u00a0
    5. \n
  5. Select\u00a0Add Recipient\u00a0and enter the email addresses of the test email recipients.\u00a0
    6. \n
  6. Select\u00a0Run Health Check.\u00a0
    7. \n
\n
Monitoring Progress
\n
To check the status of a health check, follow the steps below:
\n
    \n
  1. Select the health check line item.\u00a0
    2. \n
  2. Select\u00a0Refresh to see updated progress.\u00a0
    3. \n
\n
Results
\n
If all tests pass successfully, no further action is required. Any failed tests will be highlighted and may need admin attention.
\n
\n
\nNote:If a test email fails, our system automatically initiates a retry process. This error typically occurs due to Microsoft 365 propagation delays. We'll retry sending the test email every five minutes for two hours. This process stops when the test email is successful or the two-hour window expires.\u00a0\n
\n
\n
This approach ensures your environment is properly configured for Defend deployment while preventing excessive email traffic. If propagation times are unusually long, you may filter these test emails into a separate folder.\u00a0
\n
\n
\n
