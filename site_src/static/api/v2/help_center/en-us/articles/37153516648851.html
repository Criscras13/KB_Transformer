\nNote:Completing courses on the KnowBe4 Academy now counts as credit for the Security Awareness and Cultural Professional\u2122 (SACP\u2122) Continuing Professional Education (CPE) recertification. For more information, see the Security Awareness and Culture Professional (SACP) Certification Handbook from H Layer Credentialing.
\n
\n
The KnowBe4 Academy is an education platform where admins can learn how to successfully use and integrate KnowBe4\u2019s products. In the academy, admins can follow various learning paths for different areas of KnowBe4, including KSAT, PhishER, and SecurityCoach. If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions about the academy, reach out to academy@knowbe4.com.
\n
Accessing the Academy
\n
Before accessing the academy for the first time, ensure your first and last name is entered on the admin Profile page.
\n
To access the academy from your KSAT console, click your email address in the top-right corner of the page. Then, click KnowBe4 Academy.
\n
  
\n
To access the academy from your PhishER console, click the user icon at the bottom of the navigation bar. Then, click KnowBe4 Academy.
\n
  
\n
Setting Up Your Profile
\n
We recommend setting up your profile to customize your settings and preferences.
\n
To set up your profile, follow the steps below:
\n
    \n
  1. Log in to the KnowBe4 Academy.
    2. \n
  2. Click the profile picture icon in the top-right corner of the page, then click Settings.\n
     
    \n
    3. \n
  3. You\u2019ll be taken to the User Settings page.
    4. \n
  4. On the Profile subtab, you can edit your profile picture, personal details, and more.
    5. \n
  5. When you\u2019re finished editing information on this subtab, click Save.
    6. \n
\n
Navigating the Academy
\n
You can navigate through the academy to see our courses for our different products. For more information, see the subsections below.
\n
Home
\n
On the Home page, you can view our introductory courses, such as KnowBe4 101 and Security Awareness Training.
\n
From this page, you can also view our Knowledge Base, connect on the KnowBe4 Community, or go to KnowBe4.com.
\n
 
\n
Topics
\n
On the Topics page, you can view all of your learning topics organized by category.
\n
  
\n
When you click on a specific topic, you\u2019ll be able to view all the courses for that topic. You can click Follow to receive notifications when new lessons are added to the topic.
\n
  
\n
Library
\n
On the Library page, you can view all of your academy content.
\n
  
\n
Click on a specific activity to begin learning.
