If you have begun testing a feature that is currently in beta, you may need a Knowledge Base account to view our articles for the beta feature. Read the sections below for more information on how to register for a Knowledge Base account.
\n\n
Creating an Account
\n
To view articles about features that are currently in beta, you will need to create a Knowledge Base account. See below for more information on how to create a Knowledge Base account.
\n
    \n
  1. Navigate to https://support.knowbe4.com/hc/en-us.
    2. \n
  2. Click the Sign in button in the top-right corner of the page. \n\n
    3. \n
  3. Click Sign up next to the \u201cNew to KnowBe4?\u201d text. \n\n
    4. \n
  4. Enter your name and the email address you want to use.
    5. \n
  5. Click the Sign up button.
    6. \n
\n
You will receive an email with a link and details on how to create a password.
\n\n\n
Logging In to the Knowledge Base\n
\n
See below for information on how to log in to our Knowledge Base so that you can access beta articles.
\n
    \n
  1. Navigate to https://support.knowbe4.com/hc/en-us.
    2. \n
  2. Click the Sign in button in the top-right corner of the page. \n\n
    3. \n
  3. Once you\u2019ve entered your login information, beta articles will now be visible.
    4. \n
\n\n\n
Resetting Your Password\n
\n
If you've forgotten your password, see the information below on how to reset the password for your Knowledge Base account.
\n
    \n
  1. Navigate to https://support.knowbe4.com/hc/en-us.
    2. \n
  2. Click the Sign in button in the top-right corner of the page. \n\n
    3. \n
  3. Click\u00a0Forgot my password. \n\n
    4. \n
  4. Enter your email address and click Submit. \n\n
    5. \n
\n
If your email is registered with a valid Knowledge Base account, you will receive an email with a link and details on how to reset your password.
