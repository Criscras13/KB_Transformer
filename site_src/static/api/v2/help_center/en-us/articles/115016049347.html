KCM GRC's account settings area is where you can configure and customize several aspects of your platform. For instance, you can upload your organization's logo, choose a custom banner color for your email notifications, create custom categories for your Risk Register, and configure single sign-on\u00a0for your account.
\n
Navigating to Your Account Settings
\n
There are six subtabs available on the View Account page of your KCM GRC platform. Each subtab offers different settings for your account.\u00a0
\n
To navigate to the View Account page, go to the top-right corner of the screen and click Settings > Account Settings, as shown below.
\n
On the Account Overview subtab of the View Account page, you can see general information about your account. If you have questions about your account or if you need to modify the information on this subtab, please contact your Customer Success Manager or our support team.
\n
See the following sections to learn about the additional subtabs of settings that are available on the View Account page.
\n
Account Settings
\n
From the Account Settings subtab, you can edit the settings for your account.
\n
See the images and descriptions below for an explanation of the settings that are available under the Account Settings subtab.
\n
\n
\nImportant: If you make changes to the following settings, be sure to click the Save button at the bottom-right side of the Account Settings section.

Tags
\n
From the Tags subtab, you can create tags to categorize similar items in your account.
\n
You can create tags for the following items in KCM GRC: templates, requirements, scopes, scope requirements, controls, and risks.\u00a0
\n
Follow the steps below to create a new tag:
\n
    \n
  1. Click the Create button toward the top-right side of the Tags subtab.\u00a0
    2. \n
  2. In the Tag Name field, enter your tag. Tags can have a maximum of 25 characters.
    3. \n
  3. Click the Type\u00a0drop-down menu and select the type of item that this tag will be used for.\u00a0
    4. \n
\n
To add a new tag to an existing item in your account, navigate to the item (such as a scope, template, or control), then click the Update button. From the Tags field, you can select or search for tags that match the item type.\u00a0
\n
\n
\nException: To add a tag to a scoped requirement, navigate to the scoped requirement, then click the Add Tags button.
\n
\n
Risk Settings
\n
By default, there are six categories included in your\u00a0Risk Register. If you would like, you can add custom categories to your Risk Register from the Risk Settings subtab.
\n
\n
\nNote: The Risk Settings subtab is only available to accounts that include the Risk Management module. This module is available through our Gold and Platinum subscriptions. To learn more, please see our Risk Management Module Guide, or contact your Customer Success Manager.
\n
\n
From the View Account page, click the Risk Settings subtab, as shown below.
\n
To learn how to manage your custom categories, see the details outlined below:
\n\n
    \n
  1. \nAdd a Category: Type the name of your category into the Category Name\u00a0field; add a description in the Category Description field; then, click the +Add button.
    2. \n
  2. \nDelete a Category: Click the trashcan icon next to the category you want to delete.\u00a0
    3. \n
  3. \nEdit a Category: Click the pencil icon next to the category that you wish to edit.
    4. \n

SSO Settings
\n
The KCM GRC platform supports SAML 2.0, so your users can quickly and easily log in to KCM using your organization's single sign-on (SSO) or identity provider (IdP) without having to set up or use a password.
\n
The\u00a0SSO Settings subtab is where you will find the information that you need to set up SAML with your SSO provider. You will also need to add your SSO provider's information to this area to configure and test the SAML authentication.\u00a0
\n\n
To see the full instructions for setting up SAML or SSO for your KCM GRC account, please refer to one of the following resources:\u00a0
