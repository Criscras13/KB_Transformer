Follow the steps below to configure single sign-on (SSO) with Google Workspace. Enabling SSO will allow your users to automatically log in to the KSAT console for their security awareness training using their Google account.
\n
\n
\nNote: Make sure the email address that your users use to authenticate with SAML is either entered into the Email field or the Email Aliases field in their user profile. However, only the email address listed in the Email field will receive training notification emails. For more information about adding information to user profiles, see our User Profile Guide.
\n
\n
To configure SSO with Google Workspace, follow the steps below:
\n
    \n
  1. Log in to https://admin.google.com and select Apps.\n
    \n
    2. \n
  2. Select Web and mobile apps.\n
    \n
    3. \n
  3. Click the Add App drop-down menu, and select Search for apps.\n
    \n
    4. \n
  4. Search for \"KnowBe4\" and click Select on the app with Web (SAML) as the platform.\n
    \n
    5. \n
  5. Click Download Metadata. Keep the information from this file accessible. You'll need the SHA256 fingerprint and SSO URL found in this file for step 9. Click Continue.\n
    \n
    6. \n
  6. Fill out the fields with the appropriate information specified below. Enable Signed Response by selecting the check box, then click Continue.\n
      \n
    • \nACS URL: Enter this URL. To obtain this URL from your KSAT console, follow the steps below:\n
        \n
      • Log in to your KSAT console.
        • \n
      • Click your email address in the top-right corner and select Account Settings.
        • \n
      • Navigate to the SAML section of your settings and copy the Callback Link, such as https://training.knowbe4.com/auth/saml/xxxxxxxxxxxx/callback.
        • \n
      \n
      • \n
    • \nEntity ID: Enter \"KnowBe4\". If a unique entity ID was generated for your KSAT console, use the ID shown in your KSAT Account Settings page.
      • \n
    • \nStart URL: Enter this URL. To obtain this URL from your KSAT console, follow the steps below:\n
        \n
      • Log in to your KSAT console.
        • \n
      • Click your email address in the top-right corner, then select Account Settings.
        • \n
      • Navigate to the SAML section of your settings and copy the Sign in URL, such as https://training.knowbe4.com/auth/saml/xxxxxxxxxxxx.
        • \n
      \n
      • \n
    • \nSigned response: Select this check box.
      • \n
    • \nName ID Format: Enter \"EMAIL\".
      • \n
    • \nName ID: Choose Basic Information \u2013 Primary Email.\n
      \n
      • \n
    \n
    7. \n
  7. Under Attributes, navigate to the Basic Information section and select Primary Email. Then, click Finish.\n
    \n
    8. \n
  8. Click User access and select ON for everyone under Service status. Then, click Save.\n
    \n
    9. \n
  9. Follow the instructions listed in our Set Up SAML Single Sign-on for the Security Awareness Training Console article to complete the SAML configuration.\n
    \n
    \nNote: Google\u2019s Identity Provider (IdP) metadata file includes your SHA-256 fingerprint. Confirm that you input that metadata into KnowBe4\u2019s SAML platform.
    \n
