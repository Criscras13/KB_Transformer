From the Dashboard tab, you can monitor your organization's activity for the features that you use in your KnowBe4 account. If you have access to the widgetized dashboard feature, you can customize your default dashboard by adding, removing, resizing, and rearranging widgets. In addition to the default widgets, you can access more widgets so that you can customize your widgetized dashboard's display. For information about the default widgets that display on your dashboard, see our Dashboard article.
\n
\n
\nNote:The widgetized dashboard is only available for paid accounts that have access to both phishing and training. For more information, please contact your Customer Success Manager.
\n
\n
Additional Widgets
\n
If you have access to the widgetized dashboard, you can add additional widgets to your dashboard. Depending on how you want to display your organization's information, you can use these widgets as an alternative to some of the default widgets. To learn how to add widgets to your dashboard, see the Adding Widgets section below.
\n
\n
\nNote:Depending on your security role, you may not have access to all widgets. The widgets that you are unable to access will appear grayed out. If you hover your mouse over a widget that is grayed out, a message will display that indicates that you do not have access to the widget. For more information, please contact your administrator.
\n
\n
See the list below to learn about the additional widgets that you can add to your dashboard.
\n
    \n
  • \nPhish Alert Button Data: You can add the Phish Alert Button Data widget to view the number of times that your users have installed the PAB, uninstalled the PAB, reported a simulated phishing email with the PAB, and reported a non-simulated phishing email with the PAB. This widget can be used as an alternative to the full Phish Alert Button widget, which also includes a graph comparing your organization's phish alert reports.\n
    \n
    • \n
  • \nPhishing Industry Benchmark Data: You can add the Phishing Industry Benchmark Data widget to compare your organization\u2019s Account Average Phish-prone Percentage to an industry-specific Phish-prone Percentage. You can customize this data by changing the Industry, Organization Size, and Program Maturity from the drop-down menus. This widget can be used as an alternative to the full Phishing widget, which also includes a graph of your organization's phishing security test history.\n
    \n
    • \n
  • \nAvailable Range: You can add the Available Range widget to view your organization\u2019s current Risk Score on a scale that ranges from 0 to 100. You can use this widget as an alternative to the full Organization's Risk Score widget, which also includes a graph of your organization's risk score over time.\n
      
    \n
    \n
    \nNote:For more detailed insights into your organization\u2019s Risk Score, see our SmartRisk Agent\u2122 and Risk Score Guide.
    \n
    \n
    • \n
  • \nSAPA Industry Benchmark Data: You can add the SAPA Industry Benchmark Data widget to compare your organization\u2019s average Security Awareness Proficiency Assessment score to an industry-specific average. You can use this widget as an alternative to the full Security Awareness Proficiency Assessment (SAPA) widget, which also includes a graph breaking down the benchmark data by the SAPA knowledge area.\n
    \n
    • \n
  • \nSCS Industry Benchmark Data: You can add the SCS Industry Benchmark Data widget to compare your organization\u2019s average Security Culture Survey score to an industry-specific average. You can use this widget as an alternative to the full Security Culture Survey (SCS) widget, which also includes a graph breaking down the benchmark data by the SCS dimensions.\n
    \n
    • \n
\n
Adding Widgets
\n
If your dashboard does not display all of the widgets that you need, you can add widgets to your dashboard. You can also add widgets back to your dashboard after you remove them.
\n
Follow the instructions below to add a widget to your dashboard.
\n
    \n
  1. Log in to your KSAT account.
    2. \n
  2. From the Dashboard tab, click the grid button to edit your dashboard.\n
    \n
    3. \n
  3. Click the Add Widget button.\n
    \n
    4. \n
  4. In the Add Dashboard Widget window, select one or more of the widgets that you want to add.
    5. \n
  5. Click the Add button.\n
    \n
    6. \n
  6. Click the Save button in the top-right corner of the page.
    7. \n
\n
Removing Widgets
\n
You can remove widgets from your dashboard to focus on the widgets that are most important to your organization. For example, if your organization is not using SAPA yet, you may want to remove the Security Awareness Proficiency Assessment (SAPA) widget from your dashboard until your organization begins using the assessment.
\n
Follow the instructions below to remove a widget from your dashboard.
\n
    \n
  1. Log in to your KSAT account.
    2. \n
  2. From the Dashboard tab, click the grid button to edit your dashboard.\n
    \n
    3. \n
  3. In the top-right corner of the widget, click the minus icon to remove the widget.\n
    \n
    4. \n
  4. Click the Save button in the top-right corner of the page.
    5. \n
\n
Resizing Widgets
\n
You can adjust the sizes of widgets to emphasize specific widgets, to fit widgets next to each other, or to organize widgets in a way that helps you understand your organization's data. For example, if you want to focus on your users' training progress more than how they are using the PAB, you can make the Training widget larger than the Phish Alert Button widget.
\n
Follow the instructions below to resize a widget on your dashboard.
\n
    \n
  1. Log in to your KSAT account.
    2. \n
  2. From the Dashboard tab, click the grid button to edit your dashboard.\n
    \n
    3. \n
  3. Hover your mouse over the bottom-right corner of the widget until you see the arrow icon.
    4. \n
  4. Click and drag inwards or outwards until the widget is your preferred size.\n
    \n
    5. \n
  5. Click the Save button in the top-right corner of the page.
    6. \n
\n
Rearranging Widgets
\n
You can move widgets to different areas of your dashboard to group features together or to monitor related data. For example, if you want to determine whether your organization's Phish-prone Percentage is causing the change in your organization's Risk Score, you can place the Phishing widget next to the Risk Score Scale widget.
\n
Follow the instructions below to rearrange a widget on your dashboard.
\n
    \n
  1. Log in to your KSAT account.
    2. \n
  2. From the Dashboard tab, click the grid button to edit your dashboard.\n
    \n
    3. \n
  3. Hover your mouse over the center of a widget until you see the crossed arrow icon.
    4. \n
  4. Click and drag the widget in any direction until the widget is at your preferred location.\n
    \n
    5. \n
  5. Click the Save button in the top-right corner of the page.
    6. \n
