In the Policy Management module of KCM GRC, you can export data to view and keep records of information that you may need in the future. You can export a list of all policies, a list of all campaigns, or a report for an individual campaign in your platform. When you export data in the Policy Management module, the data exports will appear on the Data Exports page. From the\u00a0Data Exports page, you can download data exports to your device.\u00a0
\n
See the sections below for information about each of these data exports.\u00a0
\n
\n
Exporting a Policy List
\n
You can export a list of all of the policies in your platform. Exporting a policy list can help you monitor and record the policies that your users are acknowledging.\u00a0
\n
To export and download a list of your policies, follow the instructions below:
\n
    \n
  1. Navigate to\u00a0Policy Management >\u00a0Policies from the navigation panel.
    2. \n
  2. Click the\u00a0Export CSV button.\u00a0 \n
    3. \n
  3. In the Name of Export field, enter a name for your data export.\u00a0
    4. \n
  4. Click the\u00a0Create Export button.\u00a0
    5. \n
  5. Navigate to\u00a0Data Exports from the navigation panel.\u00a0
    6. \n
  6. Find the data export that you created.\n
    \n
    \nNote:In the Type column of the Data Exports page, policy list exports are labeled Policy List.\u00a0
    \n
    \n
    7. \n
  7. In the Actions column, click the Download button. \n
    8. \n
\n
After you download your policy list export, you can open the file in your preferred spreadsheet program. To learn about the types of data that the policy list export will include, see the table below:\u00a0
\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n
Column HeaderDescription
NameThis column shows the name of each policy.
TypeThis column shows Document for policies that were uploaded to your platform as a file and Link for policies that were added to your platform as a link.\u00a0
CampaignsThis column shows the campaigns that each policy is included in.
VersionThis column shows each policy's version identifier, if applicable.
DescriptionThis column shows each policy's description, if applicable.
Date CreatedThis column shows the date that each policy was added to your platform.
Last UpdatedThis column shows the date that each policy was last updated in your platform.
\n
\n
Exporting a Campaign List
\n
You can export a list of all of the campaigns in your platform. Exporting a campaign list can help you monitor and record the progress of your campaigns.\u00a0\u00a0
\n
To export and download a list of your campaigns, follow the instructions below:
\n
    \n
  1. Navigate to\u00a0Policy Management >\u00a0Campaigns from the navigation panel.
    2. \n
  2. Click the\u00a0Export CSV button.\u00a0 \n
    3. \n
  3. In the Name of Export field, enter a name for your data export.\u00a0
    4. \n
  4. Click the\u00a0Create Export button.\u00a0
    5. \n
  5. Navigate to\u00a0Data Exports from the navigation panel.\u00a0
    6. \n
  6. Find the data export that you created.\n
    \n
    \nNote:In the Type column of the Data Exports page, campaign list exports are labeled Policy Campaign List.\u00a0
    \n
    \n
    7. \n
  7. In the Actions column, click the\u00a0Download button. \n
    8. \n
\n
After you download your campaign list, you can open the file in your preferred spreadsheet program. To learn about the types of data that the campaign list export will include, see the table below:
\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n
Column HeaderDescription
Campaign NameThis column shows the name of each campaign.
PoliciesThis column shows the names of the policies that are included in each campaign.
GroupsThis column shows the user groups that are assigned to each campaign.
Auto EnrollThis column shows Yes for campaigns that have automatic enrollment enabled and No for campaigns that do not have automatic enrollment enabled.\u00a0\n
To learn about policy campaign settings, see our How to Create and Manage Policy Campaigns article.
\n
StatusThis column shows Active for campaigns that are in progress and Completed for campaigns that have ended.
Campaign OwnerThis column shows the email address of the user who created each campaign.\u00a0
Start DateThis column shows the date and time that each campaign started.\n
\n
\nImportant: The CSV file uses Universal Time Coordinated (UTC) time, so the dates and times in this column may differ from the dates and times that appear in your console. To learn about the time zone setting in your platform, see the Account Settings section of our How to Manage Your KCM GRC Account Settings article.\u00a0
\n
\n
Date CompletedThis column shows the date that each campaign was completed, if applicable.
ProgressThis column shows the percentage of policies that users have acknowledged for each campaign.
Scheduled NotificationsThis column shows the types of notifications that the campaign owner scheduled for each campaign. If there are no scheduled notifications for a campaign, this column will show No Notifications.
\n
\n
Exporting a Campaign Report
\n
You can export information about an individual campaign in your platform. Exporting a campaign report can help you view and record data about the campaign.\u00a0

    6. \n
  6. Navigate to\u00a0Data Exports from the navigation panel.\u00a0
    7. \n

  8. In the Actions column, click the\u00a0Download button.\u00a0 \n
