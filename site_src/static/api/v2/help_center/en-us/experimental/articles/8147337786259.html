The Managed Module Store, or the Managed ModStore, is a storefront that contains all the training content that we offer. The Managed ModStore works like the standard ModStore but is tailored to partners that manage accounts. You can use the Managed ModStore to add content to your managed library. Then, you can use your managed library to add specific content to the libraries of your managed accounts.
\n
Click the jump links below to learn more about the Managed ModStore and how to share content with your managed accounts. For general information about the ModStore, see our ModStore and Library Guide.
\n
Your Managed Library
\n
On the Library tab of your Managed ModStore, you can view all the modules, games, videos, and other training content you have added from the Managed ModStore. To access your managed library, log in to your KSAT management console. Then, navigate to ModStore > Library.
\n
For more information about your managed library, see the details below:
\n
\n
    \n
  1. \nStatus Filter: Click this drop-down menu to filter content by status. You can select All, Active, Retired, or Archived. You can also choose to show or hide items that have been hidden by turning on the Show Hidden Items toggle.
    2. \n
  2. \nContent Type: Click this drop-down menu to filter content by the type of content you would like to view. You can choose from video modules, games, posters, and more.
    3. \n
  3. \nSearch: Find specific content by entering a keyword used in the content title.
    4. \n
  4. \nHeaders: Sort your library by clicking on whichever table header you would like to sort by. You can sort by Content Title, Content Type, Status, Date Added, or Duration.
    5. \n
  5. \nItem Details:  Click this icon to go to the content's Item Details page in the ModStore.
    6. \n
  6. \nAdd to Managed Account:  Click this icon to add the selected content to the libraries of one or more of your managed accounts. Once you click this icon, you will be able to select which managed accounts you would like to add the content to.\n
    \n
    \nNote: You will not be able to add hidden, retired, or archived content to the libraries of your managed accounts. To show content that has been hidden, click the More Options button. Then, turn on the Show Content toggle to show the content. For more information about retired or archived content, see our ModStore and Library Guide.
    \n
    \n
    7. \n
  7. \nMore Options:  You can click this icon to open a drop-down menu with more options, including the Remove from Library button and the Hide Item toggle.
    8. \n
\n
Add Content to Your Managed Library
\n
To add content to the libraries of managed accounts, you will first need to add the content to your managed library. You can add any content to your managed library, but managed accounts can only access content that meets their subscription level.
\n
To add content to your managed library, follow the steps below:
\n
    \n
  1. Log in to your KSAT management console and navigate to the ModStore tab.
    2. \n
  2. Browse your Managed ModStore to find a piece of content that you would like to add to your library.
    3. \n
  3. Click the Add to Library button on the content\u2019s card or Item Details page. If you\u2019ve already added the content to your library, you\u2019ll see the Already Added tag.
    4. \n
\n
\n
Add Content to the Libraries of Your Managed Accounts
\n
Once you add content to your managed library, you can add it to the libraries of your managed accounts. You can use this feature to add newly released content to all eligible managed accounts at once.
\n
To add content to the libraries of your managed accounts, follow the steps below:
\n
    \n
  1. Log in to your KSAT management console and select the ModStore tab.
    2. \n
  2. In your Managed ModStore, select the Library tab.
    3. \n
  3. Locate the content you would like to add to the libraries of your managed accounts.
    4. \n
  4. On the right side of the page, click the plus sign (+) to open the Add to Managed Accounts modal.\n
    \n
    5. \n
  5. In the modal that opens, select the managed accounts you would like to add this content to. You can search for specific managed accounts by entering the organization name or primary domain into the Select one or more managed accounts\u2026 field.\n
    \n
    \nNote: The list will only include managed accounts that meet the content's subscription level and do not have the content in their library.
    \n
    \n
    6. \n
  6. Once you have selected all the managed accounts you want, click OK. A success message will display to confirm that the content was added.Once you have added content to a managed account\u2019s library, the content will display in their library along with any content added by the account\u2019s admins.\n
    \n
    7. \n
