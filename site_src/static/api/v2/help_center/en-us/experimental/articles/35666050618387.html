The dashboard screen provides an overview of the email activity observed by Defend. This information is displayed in easy-to-read graphs and charts. A side menu also allows you to navigate to other areas of the portal.
\n
\n
Navigation Menu
\n
By selecting the navigation menu at the top-left corner of the page, you can navigate to other KnowBe4 products, including Prevent, PhishER, and KSAT. For more information, see our Login Page and Navigation Menu Overview article.
\n
\n
Timeline
\n
By default, the timeline displayed is two weeks before the current date. This section can be altered at the top-right corner of the screen with the following settings:
\n
    \n
  • Day
    • \n
  • Week
    • \n
  • Month
    • \n
  • Quarter
    • \n
  • Year
    • \n
  • Custom Range
    • \n
\n
Email Totals
\n
At the top of the Dashboard screen, a selection of boxes displays counts and trends for the selected timeline.
\n
The Dangerous Emails\u00a0and\u00a0Suspicious Emails\u00a0options are clickable. Selecting either of them will take you to a filtered view of the\u00a0Recent Emails\u00a0page and show you the individual emails detected in the time period. You can select these emails to see more information.
\n
\n
The\u00a0Graymail Emails\u00a0and\u00a0Spam Emails\u00a0options show the number of emails being flagged in the selected timeframe and are clickable cards. Selecting either of them will take you to a filtered view of the\u00a0Recent Emails page and show you the individual emails detected in the time period. You can select these emails to see more information.\u00a0
\n
The\u00a0Inbound Emails\u00a0and\u00a0Outbound Emails\u00a0options show the number of emails sent and received by users whose email is being processed by Defend.\u00a0
\n
\n
Dangerous or Suspicious Emails Received
\n
This graph displays the count of emails classed as Dangerous or Suspicious in real time for the selected timeframe. The data points are interactive. Clicking on them will take you to a filtered view in Recent Emails.
\n
\n
Graymail or Spam\u00a0
\n
This graph displays the count of emails classed as Graymail or Spam in real time for the selected timeframe. The data points are interactive, clicking on them will take you to a filtered view in\u00a0Recent Emails.
\n
\n
Threat Feed
\n
The Threat Feed displays events related to Dangerous or Suspicious emails and provides a synopsis of activity. For example, 'User has clicked a link in a Dangerous email'.
\n
Select the arrow on the right of the activity to go to a filtered view of Recent Emails for further analysis.
\n
\n
Types of Phishing Emails Received
\n
This graph displays the various types of phishing emails detected by Defend. Clicking on the question mark icon provides more information about the different classifications of emails.
\n
\n
Types of Payloads in Phishing Emails
\n
This graph shows the different types of payloads identified in phishing emails detected by Defend. Clicking on the question mark icon will provide more information.
\n
\n
Supply Chain Health
\n
This graph shows the current timeline's DMARC status of all received emails. If your suppliers implement DMARC, it improves their security posture by reducing the risk of their addresses being spoofed. Clicking the question mark icon provides more information about the different statuses.
\n
\n
